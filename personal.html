Amidst the long list of life's responsibilities, I still make time to work on my own personal projects. This page details them, including both my passion in math and my efforts put into Youtube.

On February 4th, 2021, I started my youtube channel, Cubeacabra. I create videos about skill toys, specifically Rubik's Cubes and kendamas. I have learned a lot throughout these past 2 years, in terms of my skills in these toys, video editing, and hard work. I do my best to upload weekly, and produce quality, enjoyable content for all my subscribers.

All throughout school, math has always been my favorite subject. Due to this fact, I jumped on the opportunity when I found out there was a club at school that allows you to participate in math competitions. I practice calculus in my own time in order to succeed, and have been awarded for my efforts.
